Student Goals


Life Lessons


We give our young adults many tasks and challenges, but the most important thing we hope they learn are life lessons.

Simply expressed, a life lesson assists us in not repeating the same error. Life experiences can aid in improving our consciousness.

We can get understanding into how we think, act, and react in specific situations since the lesson is taught by the results of our actions or mistakes.

Perhaps one of the biggest lessons we can ultimately learn is that while we can't control everything, we can control our response to things.


Good For Learning


Engineering is excellent in helping you make mistakes that can assist in your learning, in other words (Trial & Error)

Many teachers here at Edison have expressed that engineering builds character in a student to help them develop into an adult
